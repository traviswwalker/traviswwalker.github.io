Reading

Suggested Reading and Reference

The following publications are recommended reading and reference in preparation for dissertation research in the Walker Research Group.

Additive Manufacturing

  • S.D. Hoath (ed). Fundamentals of Inkjet Printing: The Science of Inkjet and Droplets. Wiley (2016). Publisher Website
  • B. Redwood, F. Schoffer, B. Garret. The 3D Printing Handbook: Technologies, design and applications. 3D Hubs (2017). Publisher Website

Interfaces

  • J.C. Berg. An Introduction to Interfaces and Colloids: The Bridge to Nanoscience. World Scientific (2009). Publisher Website
  • P-G. de Gennes, F. Brochard-Wyar, D. Quere. Capillarity and Wetting Phenomena: Drops, Bubbles, Pearls, Waves. Springer (2004). Publisher Website

Mathematics

  • M. Abramowitz, I.A. Stegun. Handbook of Mathematical Functions: with Formulas, Graphs, and Mathematical Tables. Dover (1965). Publisher Website; NIST DLMF
  • W.E. Boyce, R.C. DiPrima. Elementary Differential Equations and Boundary Value Problems. Wiley (2009). Publisher Website
  • M. de Berg, O. Cheong, M. van Kreveld, M. Overmars. Computational Geometry. Springer (2008). Publisher Website, Author Website
  • I.S. Gradshteyn, I.M. Ryzhik. Table of Integrals, Series, and Products. Eds. A. Jeffry, D. Zwillinger. Academic Press (2000). Publisher Website
  • C. Pozrikidis. Boundary integral and singularity methods for linearized viscous flow. Cambridge University Press (1992). Publisher Website

Polymers

  • P-G. de Gennes. Scaling Concepts in Polymer Physics. Cornell University Press (1979). Publisher Website
  • M. Doi, S.F. Edwards. The Theory of Polymer Dynamics. Oxford University Press (1990). Publisher Website
  • J.D. Ferry. Viscoelastic Properties of Polymers. Wiley (1980). Publisher Website
  • P.J. Flory. Principles of Polymer Chemistry. Cornell University Press (1953). Publisher Website
  • W-M. Kulicke, C. Clasen. Viscosimetry of Polymers and Polyelectrolytes. Springer (2004). Publisher Website

Rheology

  • G.G. Fuller. Optical Rheometry of Complex Fluids. Oxford University Press (1995). Publisher Website
  • E.M. Furst, T.M. Squires. Microrheology. Oxford University Press (2017). Publisher Website
  • R.G. Larson. The Structure and Rheology of Complex Fluids. Oxford University Press (1998). Publisher Website
  • C.W. Macosko. Rheology: Principles, Measurements, and Applications. Wiley (1994). Publisher Website
  • T.G. Mezger. Applied Rheology -- With Joe Flow on Rheology Road. Anton Paar (2015). Publisher Website

Transport Phenomena

  • G.K. Batchelor. An Introduction to Fluid Dynamics. Cambridge University Press (2000). Publisher Website
  • R.B. Bird, W.E. Stewart, E.N. Lightfoot. Transport Phenomena. Wiley (2007). Publisher Website
  • L.G. Leal. Advanced Transport Phenomena: Fluid Mechanics and Convective Transport Processes. Cambridge University Press (2010). Publisher Website
