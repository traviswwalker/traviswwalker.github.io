  • 2014: Enhanced Particle Removal of Algae-Laden Surfaces Using Viscoelastic Fluids
    • Seth Gwin, Eric Eichenbaum, & Solomon Levinrad
    • Impinging jets of viscoelastic fluids have recently been demonstrated to enhance particle removal from surfaces. This team would design and build a recycling jet to remove particulate from a surface in a particular application, e.g. algae from a ship hull. Early conceptual designs feature the jet of highly elastic fluid discharged from the center of a cylindrical cross-section and siphoned to a recycle system.
