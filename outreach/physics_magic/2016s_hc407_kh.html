2016: Fishbowl and other Spinners

Katherine Hercher

The push for STEM, which stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math, is needed in this day and age more than ever before. Getting kids interested in STEM is important for their mental development and problem solving skills. My interest was starting at the youngest levels and getting them interested. I believe this is a fun activity for children, and that it is an activity that will open their minds to the creativity used in STEM.
